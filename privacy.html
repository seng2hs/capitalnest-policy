CapitalNest Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

CapitalNest ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how CapitalNest collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. CapitalNest is designed with a Privacy First approach. Your financial information is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

Information We Collect

Information You Enter

This information is stored only on your device:

  • Fixed Deposits & Recurring Deposits
  • Lending Records & Borrowing Records
  • Income, Expenses, and Notes
  • Categories & Reminder Settings
  • App Preferences

Device Information

The application may access the following parameters only to provide requested features:

  • Notification permission: For firing active system app reminders.
  • Biometric authentication: For fingerprint and face unlocking protection.
  • Local storage: To process secure database backups and restorations.

Advertising (Free Version)

The Free version may display Google AdMob advertisements. Google may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device Information
  • Approximate Location
  • Ad Performance Metrics

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle ad tracking profiles.

Purchases

If you purchase CapitalNest Pro, payments are securely processed through Google Play Billing. CapitalNest never stores or logs your raw payment details.

Analytics

Currently: No personal tracking metrics or behavioral analytics are collected. Future versions may implement completely anonymous crash reporting infrastructure to explicitly trace application health and improve execution.

Data Storage

All financial data is processed and stored locally using an encrypted SQLite data bundle. No automated cloud storage configurations are enabled in Version 1.

Data Security

CapitalNest provides built-in mechanisms to protect local user instances:

  • PIN Lock configuration
  • Fingerprint & Face Authentication support
  • Local Backup and Restore modules

Data Sharing

We do not sell, distribute, leak, or share any of your transactional records or personal financial information with any entities.

Children's Privacy

CapitalNest is intended for structural utility by users over 18 years old.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time. Check back periodically to review changes.

Contact

For privacy queries, email us at: support@capitalnest.app
